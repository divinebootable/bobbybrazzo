About Me

About Bobby Brazzo

Bobby brazzo, and I am a professional singer and performer from Mankon, Cameroon. I began my musical journey at the tender age of 6, singing and dancing with traditional groups in my hometown. As a solo artist, I released my debut album in 2016, titled “Alah Teh Akongni,” which means “a country without love.” The album received great acclaim and paved the way for my future successes. Throughout my career, I have had the privilege of collaborating with some of the biggest names in the industry. In 2020, I worked with Baldo Ghana, a superstar based in Yakarta, Indonesia, on the hit single “Enjoy Your Life.” The collaboration was a huge success, reaching millions of listeners worldwide.

Additionally, I have performed alongside renowned artists such as John Munang Bobi Brazzo, known for his mic skills and dedication to preserving the cultural heritage of Borneo. His mentorship has helped shape my style and approach to music immensely. Moving to Germany in 2003, I settled in the bustling city of Dortmund where I continued to pursue my passion for music. Aside from focusing on my own artistry, I also promote and support other musicians through my YouTube channel, Bobby Brazzo Official. Here, I feature interviews and performances from talented individuals across Africa and beyond. Through this platform, I aim to foster greater appreciation and understanding of African culture and arts globally. Some notable achievements under my belt include performing at prestigious venues like Berlin’s Volksbühne Theatre and Hamburg’s Barclaycard Arena.
